Motivation for Tangible Interfaces for GIS

  • Intuitive, collaborative environments for landscape design augmented with geospatial analyses.
  • Platform for decision making and science communication where scientists and non-experts can interact.
  • Improve peoples' understanding of geospatial algorithms, processes and interactions.
  • Environment for testing geospatial algorithms during model development.



Tangible Landscape is designed to make scientific data, models, and simulations exploratory, engaging, and fun

Tangible interaction with GIS

With Tangible Landscape you can hold a GIS in your hands - feeling the shape of the earth, sculpting its topography, and directing the flow of water.
